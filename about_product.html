About Our Product

The Hydro Power Welding Machine is a functional prototype developed by DK Services Company Ltd, utilizing innovative hydro-power technology to redefine energy efficiency in welding.

Designed for reliability and performance, it optimizes power usage while maintaining a stable arc—making it ideal for diverse industrial applications. Whether it’s welding steel, aluminum, or other alloys, the compact design is engineered to handle both heavy-duty tasks and precise, small-scale jobs.

While still in its prototype stage, the machine already demonstrates consistent results. With ongoing refinements in power regulation and cooling, this welding innovation offers a glimpse into the future of affordable, energy-efficient welding.

Key Features

Hydro-Power Tech

Water-based system for energy efficiency.

Material Flexibility

Works with steel, aluminum, and alloys.

Portable Design

Lightweight (7kg), easy to transport.

Stable Arc

Consistent welds with ongoing refinements.

User-Friendly

Simple controls for precise welding adjustments.
